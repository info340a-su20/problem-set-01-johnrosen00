Donkey Kong

Donkey Kong is a big monkey. He appears in games such as Donkey Kong and Donkey Kong Jr. Math where he throws barrels at an italian plumber named Mario, among other things. He has a friend named Funky Kong.

Here are some of the reasons why I admire Donkey Kong more than anybody, more than even my father:
  • He is a big monkey.
  • He is a connoisseur of bananas.
  • He throws barrels at Mario.
